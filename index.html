focus.js

A lightweight, privacy‑first toolkit for redacting sensitive content directly in the browser.

What is focus.js?

focus.js is a simple, open‑source redaction library designed for creators, developers, and editors who need fast, local, no‑upload redaction tools. Whether you're hiding personal info, censoring screenshots, or preparing content for public release, focus.js keeps everything on your device — no servers, no tracking, no data leaving your browser.

✨ Why it matters: Redaction should be instant, private, and easy. focus.js gives you that.
